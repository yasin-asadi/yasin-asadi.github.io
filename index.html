Yasin Asadi

Biography

Yasin Asadi is a Computer Engineer, researcher, reviewer, and lecturer specializing in Machine Learning, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Network Engineering. He holds a Master of Science in Computer Architecture System Design and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering.

Currently, he teaches advanced topics at various universities, inspiring the next generation in these fields. With extensive experience in peer review for journals such as IEEE, Elsevier, IGI Global, Taylor & Francis, and Springer Nature, he is committed to advancing knowledge and innovation.

His research focuses on connecting computer engineering and artificial intelligence across disciplines, with significant contributions to machine learning, data science, and network engineering. Dedicated to advancing technology and education, he makes impactful contributions in applied sciences.
